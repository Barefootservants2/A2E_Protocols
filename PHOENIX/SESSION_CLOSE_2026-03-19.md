### Key Concepts Documented
- **Positive Gamma**: Market makers long gamma = buy dips, sell rallies = DAMPEN volatility
- **Negative Gamma**: Market makers short gamma = buy rallies, sell dips = AMPLIFY volatility
- **Gamma Flip Point**: Price where net GEX transitions positive/negative
- **Hedging Formula**: Hedge Volume = Γ × (ΔS)² — QUADRATIC scaling creates feedback loops
- **Silver Dealer Positioning**: Dealers chronically SHORT gamma because retail buys calls, dealers sell them
- **The "Manipulation" Mechanism**: Not conspiracy — Black-Scholes delta-hedging FORCES market makers to buy futures when price rises (gamma squeeze) or sell when price falls (gamma cascade). The math dictates the behavior.
- **Gamma Flip + Physical Squeeze**: When dealers are short gamma into a physically depleting market, hedging math creates a one-way door
