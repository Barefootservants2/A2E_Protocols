## PHILOSOPHY

Questions should REVEAL expertise, not ASK for it.  
Questions should TEACH while detecting.

**WRONG:** "What's your experience level?"  
**RIGHT:** "What's the null hypothesis you're testing?"

---

## FINANCE DOMAIN

### Entry Questions (Reveals Basic Understanding)

1. "What's your current thesis on this investment?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** "I heard it's going up" / No thesis
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Basic bull/bear case
   - **EXPERT:** Multi-factor thesis with catalysts

2. "What would invalidate your bull/bear case?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** Can't articulate
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Single factor
   - **EXPERT:** Multiple specific failure modes

3. "What's your position sizing methodology?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** "I'll put in $1000" / No methodology
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Percentage-based
   - **EXPERT:** Kelly Criterion / Risk-adjusted sizing

### Intermediate Questions (Reveals Depth)

4. "What's your stop-loss strategy for this position?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** No stop-loss / "I'll just hold"
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Fixed percentage
   - **EXPERT:** Volatility-adjusted / Technical levels

5. "How does this fit your portfolio allocation?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** No portfolio view
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Basic diversification
   - **EXPERT:** Correlation-aware allocation

6. "What's the catalyst timeline?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** "Whenever it goes up"
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** General timeframe
   - **EXPERT:** Specific dated catalysts

### Expert Questions (Confirms Mastery)

7. "Walk me through your due diligence process."
   - Tests systematic approach

8. "What's the insider activity telling you?"
   - Tests Form 4 literacy

9. "How are you hedging this position?"
   - Tests risk management sophistication

## ACADEMIC DOMAIN

### Entry Questions

1. "What's your research question?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** Vague topic
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Focused question
   - **EXPERT:** Testable hypothesis

2. "What methodology are you using?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** "I'll research it"
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Named methodology
   - **EXPERT:** Justified methodology choice

3. "What are your primary sources?"
   - **NOVICE SIGNAL:** Wikipedia / General search
   - **INTERMEDIATE:** Academic databases
   - **EXPERT:** Specific seminal papers

### Intermediate Questions

4. "What's your literature gap?"
   - Tests research positioning

5. "What are the limitations of your approach?"
   - Tests self-awareness

6. "How will you validate your findings?"
   - Tests methodological rigor

### Expert Questions

7. "What's your contribution to the field?"
   - Tests originality awareness

8. "Who would disagree with your thesis and why?"
   - Tests intellectual honesty

9. "What follow-up research does this enable?"
   - Tests vision
