# AT THE BOTTOM
*by William Earl Lemon*
*Genre: Delta Blues / Memphis Soul*
*Vibe: Morgan Luna x Duane Allman (Going Down Slow) x Jonny Lang (Still Raining)*
*Key: E or A minor | Tempo: ~60-65 BPM*
*Date: 2026-03-11*

---

## ARRANGEMENT NOTES
- Verse 1: Full voice, dry — single guitar note hanging, no band
- Verse 2: Band enters low — kick drum, bass, nothing else
- Chorus: Voice drops, slide guitar comes up
- Verse 3: Organ enters — one low note held
- Verse 4: Voice worn down, not angry
- Verse 5: Just organ and slide — no drums
- Outro: Voice barely above a whisper, guitar holds last note, does not resolve
- The whisper lines ARE the slide guitar answering the vocal
- "At the bottom of that bottle" — guitar only, no vocal

---

## LYRICS

**VERSE 1** *(full voice, dry)*
Some may die in shame
They cant stand the pain
Another day of the same ole thing
You pray to God but know youre insane
As the life you lived didnt follow Him

**VERSE 2** *(band enters low)*
They tell you stand up and be a man
You deal with those days the best you can
Its all in your head
They wont get it until you wind up dead

**CHORUS** *(voice drops, slide guitar comes up)*
The same old bullshit lies
*(whisper) "we tell ourselves"*
The same old ending to another day
*(whisper) "we tell ourselves"*
Tomorrow is going to be ok

---

**VERSE 3** *(organ enters — one low note held)*
Some days there is no way out
You look for help in all the wrong places
The only help you ever needed
Was to be in Gods graces

**VERSE 4** *(voice getting tired — not angry, worn)*
So you look to your only friend
*(whisper) "other than the bottle"*
He is always there
*(guitar only — no vocal — slide finishes the sentence)*
*(whisper) "at the bottom of that bottle"*

**CHORUS** *(same but heavier — you know what the friend is now)*
The same old bullshit lies
*(whisper) "we tell ourselves"*
The same old ending to another day
*(whisper) "we tell ourselves"*
Tomorrow is going to be ok

---

**VERSE 5** *(just organ and slide — no drums)*
The devil is whispering
You choose to listen
That same ole ending to another day
He was always there

*(guitar only — one slow slide down)*
*(whisper) "at the bottom"*

**OUTRO CHORUS** *(voice barely above a whisper)*
The lies have stopped
*(whisper) "so we tell ourselves"*
The same old ending to another day
*(whisper) "we tell ourselves"*
Tomorrow will be different

*(guitar holds the last note — does not resolve)*

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES
- Hidden name reveal: "the bottle" / "other than the bottle" / "at the bottom of that bottle"
- The friend is never named until Verse 4 — listener hears it land
- Final chorus shift: "going to be ok" → "will be different" — resignation dressed as hope
- Working title: AT THE BOTTOM
