## ASSAY Quick Check (5 Questions)

1. Is the analytical tool appropriate for this question?
2. Are all assumptions stated explicitly?
3. Can the claims be proven wrong?
4. Is the depth proportionate to the stakes?
5. Would three experts agree on the methodology?

**3+ YES = Proceed | 2 YES = Conditional | <2 YES = Stop**

## AUTOPSY Scan (Top 5 Red Flags)

- [ ] Absolute language for uncertain outcomes?
- [ ] Can you trace every data point to a primary source in 2 hops?
- [ ] Is the narrative driving the analysis or supporting it?
- [ ] Does every absolute number have a denominator?
- [ ] Are counter-arguments genuine or performative?

**2+ flags = Do not act until resolved**
