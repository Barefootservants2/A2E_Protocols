## 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT

The flat -5% stop-loss applied uniformly across all position types has resulted in repeated whipsaw exits during elevated volatility (VIX 20+), converting temporary drawdowns into permanent realized losses. In a market averaging 1.4% daily swings, a -5% stop triggers on normal volatility rather than thesis failure. The cumulative effect is identical to a single large loss — the money is gone, just slower. This amendment replaces the uniform stop with a tiered matrix calibrated to each ring's volatility profile and holding period.

## 3. TRIM DISCIPLINE — THE $1-BY-$1 REBUILD

The objective is not home runs. The objective is singles that stay in the scorebook. Every trim that locks in profit is ammunition for the next entry.

| Trigger | Action | Rule |
|---------|--------|------|
| **+5%** | Move stop to breakeven | Eliminate downside risk. This position can no longer hurt you. |
| **+10%** | Sell 50% of position | Lock in profit. Remaining 50% rides with house money. Cash goes to SGOV or next entry. |
| **+20%** | Sell 60% of remaining | Only 20% of original position remains. You have banked 80% of the gain. Let the rest run. |
| **Re-entry** | Fresh data ONLY | Re-entry after a trim requires a new catalyst confirmed through MICHA gates. Emotional re-entry (FOMO) is BANNED. Data leads, Principal decides. |
