## PART 2: THE SIGNAL STACK — WHAT ELITE TRADERS ACTUALLY USE

Research synthesis: Zarattini/Aziz/Barbon (Swiss Finance Institute, 2024), institutional VWAP methodology, and 17+ years of SPY backtesting data.

**The best-performing intraday traders use the same core stack:**

### Layer 1 — VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)
The institutional benchmark. Every major fund, algorithm, and execution desk references VWAP.
- Price ABOVE VWAP = bullish intraday bias. Institutions buying.
- Price BELOW VWAP = bearish bias. Institutions selling or neutral.
- VWAP cross with volume >150% of average = directional confirmation signal.
- VWAP as trailing stop: if price closes below VWAP on a long, EXIT. No debate.

**Why this matters for A2E:** This is how institutions hide their orders. HUNTER tracking VWAP alignment across our watchlist tells us where institutional money is flowing before the retail crowd sees it.

### Layer 2 — Opening Range (First 30 Minutes)
The 9:30-10:00AM range sets the day's battlefield. 
- Break ABOVE opening range high with volume = long signal.
- Break BELOW opening range low = short or exit signal.
- Strongest signals occur 9:30-11:00AM and 3:00-4:00PM.
- Mid-day (11:30AM-2:30PM) = consolidation. Reduced position sizing unless catalyst present.

### Layer 3 — Momentum Confirmation (RSI + MACD + EMA Cross)
- RSI 14: Entry zone 40-60 in trend, >50 for longs. Avoid chasing RSI >70.
- MACD: Signal line cross in direction of VWAP bias = confirmation.
- EMA 9/20 cross: Fast cross above slow = momentum building. Fast cross below = momentum dying.

### Layer 4 — Volume Profile
- Entry volume must be >130% of 20-day average for Trade 1 confirmation.
- Declining volume on a move = momentum exhausting = exit signal.
- Volume spike against position direction = hard exit, no waiting.

### Layer 5 — Catalyst (HUNTER Signal)
- Insider filing (H4/H17/H22) within 7 days.
- Congressional trade (H30) in sector within 14 days.
- Earnings catalyst (H25) or economic event (H26) within 48 hours.
- Institutional flow signal (H39) aligning with direction.
- News sentiment (H5/H16) positive and fresh (<24 hours).

**All 5 layers aligning = 95%+ confidence. This is our trade.**
**4 of 5 layers = 85-94% confidence. Reduced size.**
**3 or fewer = DO NOT TRADE.**

## PART 4: EXIT RULES — WE SELL BEFORE THE RIDE DOWN

### Hard Exit Rules (Non-Negotiable — Auto-Execute)

**Rule 1 — Hard Stop:** -2.5% on PRIME, -2.0% on STRONG, -1.5% on PROBE.
Set at order entry. No manual override. No "waiting for it to come back."

**Rule 2 — VWAP Cross Exit:** If price closes BELOW VWAP on a 5-minute candle while in a long position → EXIT. No exceptions. VWAP was the entry thesis anchor.

**Rule 3 — Two-Check Adverse Move Rule:**
If position moves against us on TWO consecutive 15-minute checks:
- Check 1: -1%+ adverse
- Check 2 (15 min later): Still declining or -0.5%+ more
- Action: EXIT at market. Do not wait for Check 3.
- **Exception:** HUNTER data explicitly says "hold" (fresh catalyst, institutional buying confirmed). This exception requires documented evidence, not a feeling.

**Rule 4 — Time Stop:** All Ring 4 positions CLOSED by 3:45PM ET. No overnight holds on tactical plays. This is the Track 1 mandate.

**Rule 5 — Profit Lock:** 
- At +5%: Sell 50% of position. Move stop on remainder to breakeven.
- At +10%: Sell 60% of remainder. Let last piece run with VWAP trailing stop.
- At +20%: Exit everything. Bank it.

### Intelligent Hold Override (The "Don't Be Stupid" Rule)
The Two-Check Rule has one exception: if HUNTER is actively showing:
- Fresh institutional buying (H39-FLOW = ACCUMULATION)
- Positive news <2 hours old (H5/H16)

Then HUNTER can issue a HOLD SIGNAL that suspends the Two-Check Rule for ONE additional check. One. Not indefinitely. After the third consecutive adverse check, EXIT regardless of HUNTER signal.
