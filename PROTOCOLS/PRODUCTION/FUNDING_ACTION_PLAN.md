## PART 1: SAM.gov REGISTRATION (DO THIS WEEK)

SAM.gov registration is **required** for ALL federal grants. It takes 2-4 weeks to process. Every day you wait is a day you can't apply.

### Step-by-Step:

**Step 1: Get a Unique Entity ID (UEI)**
- Go to: https://sam.gov
- Click: "Get Started" ‚Üí "Register Entity"
- You need:
  - Legal business name: Ashes2Echoes, LLC
  - Physical address: Newport News, VA (your registered address)
  - EIN (Employer Identification Number) ‚Äî if you have one for A2E LLC
  - If no EIN yet: Apply at https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online (instant, free)
  - DUNS number is no longer required ‚Äî UEI replaced it

**Step 2: Complete Entity Registration**
- Business type: LLC / Small Business
- SBA size standard: Small (fewer than 500 employees)
- NAICS codes to register under:
  - **541715** ‚Äî Research and Development in the Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences
  - **541511** ‚Äî Custom Computer Programming Services
  - **541512** ‚Äî Computer Systems Design Services
  - **541519** ‚Äî Other Computer Related Services
  - **518210** ‚Äî Computing Infrastructure Providers, Data Processing, Web Hosting
- Socioeconomic status: Check if you qualify for:
  - Veteran-owned small business
  - Service-disabled veteran-owned (if applicable)
  - Small disadvantaged business (8(a)) ‚Äî may qualify based on personal net worth thresholds
- Financial information: Your business bank account details

**Step 3: Wait for Validation (7-14 business days)**
- IRS validates your EIN/TIN
- SAM assigns your UEI
- You receive confirmation email
- **After confirmation: You can apply for ANY federal grant**

**Step 4: Register on Grants.gov**
- Go to: https://grants.gov
- Create account using your SAM.gov UEI
- This is where SBIR/STTR applications are submitted

## PART 3: TAX STRATEGY (Discuss with CPA)

### Section 174 ‚Äî R&D Expenses (Mandatory Capitalization)
Since 2022, R&D expenses must be CAPITALIZED and amortized over 5 years (domestic) or 15 years (foreign). This means you can't deduct them all in Year 1 ‚Äî but they ARE deductible over time.

What qualifies:
- All software development costs (your n8n workflows, code, protocols)
- API subscriptions used for R&D
- Cloud compute for model training
- Your time spent developing (at reasonable hourly rate)
- Testing and experimentation costs

### Section 41 ‚Äî R&D Tax Credit (THE BIG ONE)
Small businesses can claim up to **$250,000/year** against payroll taxes.

**Qualification test (all 4 must be met):**
1. ‚úÖ Technological in nature ‚Äî AI/ML development
2. ‚úÖ Elimination of uncertainty ‚Äî you don't know if the approach will work
3. ‚úÖ Process of experimentation ‚Äî you test, iterate, modify
4. ‚úÖ Qualified purpose ‚Äî developing new or improved functionality

**A2E qualifies on ALL FOUR.** Multi-agent orchestration, confidence cascade algorithms, ML feedback loops ‚Äî all are novel development with uncertain outcomes.

**Documentation required:**
- ‚úÖ GitHub commit history (timestamps, descriptions, changes)
- ‚úÖ Protocol version changelogs
- ‚úÖ Test harness results (213 tests documented)
- ‚úÖ Session transcripts (PHOENIX carry-forward documents)
- ‚ùì Time tracking ‚Äî START tracking hours spent on R&D

**Action:** Talk to your CPA about Form 6765 (Credit for Increasing Research Activities).

### Section 179 / Bonus Depreciation ‚Äî Equipment
